Dew Drop

Dew Drop: A Responsive Money Saving App

Have you ever wanted to be a good money saver and just haven’t known where to start? Or how to start? Good news, you’re not alone! Welcome to Dew Drop, where we shoot for achieving our financial goals and dreams one drop at a time. We understand the stress that comes from budgeting and planning, so we are here to help you every step of the way. Here at Dew Drop, we strive to support and motivate as we continue to grow in our financial goals.

Have you heard of the money tree? Although that may be a myth or even legend, we use this ideology in the core of our name. A money tree may be this grand, miraculous thing that everyone wishes they could have, but it has to come from somewhere. Like any plant, this tree would need to start as a small sprout. Each time we add dew to our little sprout, it will slowly and surely grow, one day becoming that miraculous money tree.

Goals take time, just like it takes time for the money tree to grow. Any goal in life can be achieved with the right amount of patience and effort. We understand not everyone is fluent with the world of finance, and that is okay. We are here to support everyone at any level they may be at. So with that being said, let’s get started!
